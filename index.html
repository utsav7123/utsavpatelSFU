About Me

Hi, I am Utsav Patel, a Computing Science student at Simon Fraser University. I love programming, and for me, it all starts with logic and creative problem-solving. Python is my favorite language, and I am always excited by the possibilities in artificial intelligence.

Skills and Interests

I have a wide range of experience in web development, including both front end and back end, as well as AI model training, building data pipelines, database management, and game development. I enjoy creating games to play with my friends, and every new project becomes my favorite because I always try to make it the best I can.

Background

Outside of coding, I used to be an artist and photographer before programming took over all my free time. I still bring my creative side to my projects, whether I am solving a tough problem or designing a new feature. My proudest project so far is a vacuum cleaner AI that locates and cleans dust in the most efficient way, showing how simple ideas can be turned into clever solutions.
